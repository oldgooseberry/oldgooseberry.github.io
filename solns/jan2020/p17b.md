# Question

What is

$41 + 2 - 3 \cdot 4 - 5 + 24 \div 2^{3} - 3 \cdot 2 + 6 = ?$

# Solution

{% raw %}{::nomarkdown}<div>
$\begin{align*}
41 + 2 - 3 \cdot 4 - 5 + 24 \div 2^{3} - 3 \cdot 2 + 6 &= \\
41 + 2 - 12 - 5 + 24 \div 8 - 6 + 6 &= \\
41 + 2 - 12 - 5 + 3 - 6 + 6 &= \\
43 - 12 - 2  &= \\
43 - 14  &= 29\\
\end{align*}$
